Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

NoiseRecord (“the App”) is designed to measure environmental noise and capture audio/video evidence on your device. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data stored on your device

All recordings, measurement logs, calibration settings, and exported files are stored locally on your iPhone. The App does not upload your audio, video, or measurement data to our servers.

Permissions we request

  • Microphone — Required to measure noise levels and record voice-activated audio. Audio is processed on-device for dB readings, waveforms, and optional AI sound classification.
  • Camera — Required only when you use the Video feature to record evidence with decibel overlays burned into the footage.
  • Location (When In Use) — Used only during video recording to embed GPS coordinates in the evidence overlay. Location is not tracked in the background for other features.
  • Background audio — If you enable “Background monitoring,” the App may continue measuring and voice-activated recording while in the background. This uses more battery.

Third-party services

The App does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in its current version. If you share files via the system share sheet, those actions are governed by the app or service you choose.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
